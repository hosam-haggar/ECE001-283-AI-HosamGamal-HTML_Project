Futute Aspects of Using Artificial Intelligence

AI researches have made a lot of progress in the past few decades, but it is not ready to completely replace the human mind, but combining machine learning technology with high-speed and big data processing power will lead to tremendous progress in AI field. I can summarize the future aspects of AI in the following short notes:

  • Machines and computers will be much more advanced than today,
  • Speech recognition systems will reach much higher levels of performance,
  • Expert system applications will be used in several aspects of life,
  • Robots will be faster and more efficient than today,
  • Home security is becoming smarter and may be controlled by voice,
  • AI trading systems will help get a smarter trading,
  • Robots will help companies increse productivity with minimum maintenance cost,
  • In one word, AI will shape our life in the future.
